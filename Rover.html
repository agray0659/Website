Obstacle Course Rover

Embedded system project (embedded C, PCB design, Microcontrollers)

Back to Projects

About

In this project, my team and I made a wireless controlled rover that was designed to scan the course using a ultrasonic sensor and return the information to our laptop where we had a GUI displaying a map of the scanned course, from there we could then control the rover to navigate the course.

Rover utilized a Bluetooth 9600 Baud USART component, an ultrasonic sensor, a servo, and 2 motor driver circuits to control the speed and direction of each wheel. The software for the Microcontroller utilized multple embedded systems concepts including (UART, I2C, Timers, Interrupts, GPIO, and PWM)
